Panama Metro Line 3

2021 - Present | Role: Power of Attorney (PoA)

Overseeing the EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) of a 25km monorail system with 14 stations. This project includes a 5.3km tunnel under the Panama Canal.

  • Key Role: Project Executive & Legal Representative (PoA).
  • Challenge: Managing complex interface between tunnel and viaduct sections.
  • Scale: $2.8 Billion USD (Largest infrastructure project in Panama).

2015 - 2020 | Role: Project Manager (PM)

Construction of the high-speed railway trackbed for the Honam line, enabling speeds up to 300km/h.

  • Technical: Applied advanced NATM tunneling methods and soft-ground treatment.
  • Outcome: Successful completion on schedule with zero safety incidents.

Donghae Line (National Rail)

2006 | Role: Detailed Design Engineer

Executed the Detailed Design for the Wondeok-Imwon section (Lot 15) of the Donghae Line.

  • Scope: Earthwork, drainage, and structural design for single-track railway.
  • Focus: Optimized alignment to minimize environmental impact.
  • Result: Delivered precise design drawings approved by Korea Rail Network Authority.
